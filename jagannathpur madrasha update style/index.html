Assalamualaikum Wa Rahmatullahi Owa barakatuhu

Bismillahir Rahmanir Rahim.

In world history Islamic teaching has always been a bright horizon.The foundation of prophetic teaching was laid down by Rasulullah (SAW) at the Dar-e-Arqum in Macca, the Moszid-e-Nabbi in Madina and its surrounding Suffas. After the demise of Rasulullah (SAW) his companions (R.A.) and later the Muslim scholars had preserved, and spread that teaching gradually.

The Madrasahs in Bangladesh are strongly connected with this chain, thus our madrasha (Jamia Islamia Khatune Jannat Fathima (R.) mohila madrasa) is also provide islamic teaching for female student to stablished them proper islamic education.

In 1995 Alhazz Kwari Edris Miah established "Jamia Islamia Khatune Jannat Fathima (R.) Mohila Madrasa.His intention was to make an effort in adorning society with the virtues of humanity by creating the religious consciousness among people to make them strong in morally and in character. Above all, his dream was to bring them spiritually closer to Allah.

He made the Madrasah a prosperous centre for Islamic education. From primary level up to the highest degree (Takmil Fhil Hadith (Title)) classes, he opened departments for Hifz, Tajweed, Fatwah, and other important departments. As a result, the overall social environment of the area had changed. Where there was darkness, a glow of light had enlightened the area.There are more than 250 students at present acquiring religious study and worldly knowledge with full care and attention. There are 19 well qualified teachers who teach and monitor student progress. Alhamdulillah the Madrasah is progressing day by day and moving forward to achieve its main goals.
