Our Speciality

  1. Teching provide under the complete islamic shariah system.
  2. Each student must follow the rules of islamic shariah and hizab system.
  3. The syllabus complies with the Qawmi Madrasah Education Board and national curriculum.
  4. Teaching by trained and experienced teachers.
  5. Arrange weekly debating club, seminar including hamd, nath, Telawath and competition.
  6. Marasha has student's hostel under 24 hour take care.
  7. Provide residential system to student.
  8. The weak student get most takes after by teachers.
  9. The poor, most talent and orphan(Ethim) students get more opportunity to study here.
  10. Examinations held by semester system.
  11. Hands on teaching, weekly and monthly test to improve the levels of students.
  12. Daily in-class teaching and learning (tutorial)
  13. Discussion on teaching based on weekly reports produced by teachers

Aims & Objectives

  1. To lay the foundation of Islamic education in accordance with the Q'uran and Sunnah that would be productive and suitable for this age.
  2. To follow the renowned Darul Uloom Deoband, which came in to existence with the thought and inspiration of Shah Waliullah (rah.) the pioneer of Islamic renaissance in the subcontinent.
  3. To preserve the religion of Islam, teaching and Tablig as well as A'la-e-Kalimatullah.
  4. To prepare self-dependant citizens by incorporating technical and vocational (job related) education along with the Islamic education.
  5. By following the Sunnah of Prophet (SAW) completely, creating a favourable atmosphere so that the following of Sunnah would be in accordance with the spiritually luminous way of the companions (RA.) and pious predecessors in thought, faith & belief, piety and character.
