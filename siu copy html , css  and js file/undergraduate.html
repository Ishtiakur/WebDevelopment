Undergraduate

Admissions

The admission office advises the prospective student and guardians regarding admission to SIU. Students and guardians are always welcome and encouraged to visit the campus for individual Counselling.

Undergraduate Admissions Criteria

Admissions requirements for undergraduate program are as follows: At least 2.5 grade point in each of the Secondary School Certificate(SSC) and Higher Secondary Certificate(HSC) from a board of university or equivalent examination. Diploma in Engineering from the Bangladesh Technical Education Board(BTEB) on a diploma recognized by BTEB with a GPA of 2.5 and above out of 4.0 may be admitted in the B.Sc. Engineering Program.

International Students

For the purpose of admission, internal application are defined as all persons are not citizen of Bangladesh, International applicants who seek admission at SIU, must meet the same academic standards for admission as those required of local students. Since there are wider variations in educational systems throughout the world, international aaplicants are selected on the basis of their academic work, proficiency in English and Mathematics, probability of success in the chosen curricula, and certification of adequate financial resources.

Transfer Credits

Credits earned at previously attended colleges and university will be evaluated for acceptance toward SIU degrees by faculty members. Transcripts from previously attended institutions must be submitted for evaluation. A student who wishes to seek transfer with has earned credit to other university at home and abroad may be allowed to do so. Credits earned at SIU are accepted by all university at home and abroad. Some of our student have already migrated to foreign universities and their credits earned here have been accepted.
